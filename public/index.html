What is CHECs?

Measuring the Usability of Community Buildings with the 'Community Health Environment Checklists'

It is an assessment that objectively measures usability and receptivity of public spaces for people who have disabilities and older adults

Accessibility describes the physical environment, but

Usability describes how an individual interacts with his or her environment.

A building may be accessible, but is it really usable by all?
